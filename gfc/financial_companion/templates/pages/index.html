About



Greggor will be you guide throughout this application
He'll be around to help you manage your spending and saving
You can always find him on the top hand corner of the page
He will change mood depending on how you interact with the site
This includes the date, time and how well you manage your financial accounts
To keep him happy, make sure to keep your financial life in order
Your goal should be to make sure he doesn't get upset.





Features



This is your guide to the features our product provides.
After creating an account and logging in you will be met with a dashboard which will give you a brief overview your accounts and transactions. If you have set up any bank accounts with an interest rate you will be able to view a graph of your saving projections.
At the top of the page you will see the navigation bar. This allows you to access all of the features of the website. Here is a description of each of these items:
Accounts
Manage all of your accounts here. You are able to view further information regarding each account, add new accounts, and delete or edit any existing accounts.
Transactions
Manage all of your transactions here. You are able to view further information regarding each transaction, add new transaction, and delete or edit any existing transactions.
Categories
Manage all of your categories here. You are able to view more information about each category and delete or edit delete any existing categories. When viewing a category, you can see information about the category, any targets for the category and any transactions linked to that category.
Groups
You can create or join groups in order to compete with your friends to stay within your limits and complete your goals. Each group will also have a leaderboard where you can view their position amongst your fellow members.
Quiz
You can take a quiz which will help you to improve your general financial knowledge. You can also see previous results so you can track your progress.
Targets
You can create three types of targets: account targets, category targets and user targets. Upon completion or approaching completion of any target, you will see a notification to warn you or congratulate about the targets. You can also manage all of your targets here on the targets page.
Spending Summary
You can keep track and view all of your progress regarding their spending over income. You can also see different graphs to analyse your spending.
Merchandise
Can't get enough of the lovable, heart-warming, little dude? Buy some Greggor merchandise today and wear him around so you can be just as stylish as everyone's favourite penguin.
